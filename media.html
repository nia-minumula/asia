Books, film, TV, and music worth starting with.

A mix of scholarship by professors in the field and creative work by Asian American writers, directors, and musicians. Search or filter by ethnicity, language, or genre to build your own reading and watch list.

Books/Papers · History

The Making of Asian America: A History

Erika Lee — professor, University of Minnesota

A sweeping history of Asian immigration to the U.S. from the 1800s to the present, often used as an entry-level text in intro courses.

Books/Papers · Essay

Minor Feelings: An Asian American Reckoning

Cathy Park Hong — poet & professor, Rutgers–Newark

A collection of essays on race, art, and the emotional interior of growing up Korean American, blending memoir and criticism.

Strangers from a Different Shore

Ronald Takaki — professor, UC Berkeley

A foundational text of the Asian American Studies field, tracing the histories of multiple Asian ethnic groups in the U.S. since the 1800s.
